Dreaming about visiting the Inka Empire?

Cuzco

Cuzco was once the capital of the Inka Empire, here you will find tourists during the entire year, the weather is cold and rainy specially from December to March. Due to its fame and cultural heritage, it is easy to find tour operators, money exchange offices and food. If you ever find yourself in Cuzco, make sure you stop at Mercado San Pedro to have their most famous dishes, including Ceviche. There are a lot of cheap hotels and there is always vacancy.

Machu Picchu

This city is the most familiar icon of the Inka Empire, tour operators pick you up in Cuzco and take you on a trip that includes a ride on a tourist train. The purpose of the city is still a mistery, locals believe it was once the capital of the Inka Empire or a place where royal families could rest and trade. The weather here is warm. The best time to visit it is in summer, from May to October. This city is a sacred symbol to peruvians.
