Dreaming about visiting the Inka Empire?

Cuzco

Cuzco was once the capital of the Inka Empire, here you will find tourist during the entire year, the weather is cold and rainy specially from December to March. Due to its fame and cultural heritage, it is easy to find tour operators, money exchange offices and food. If you ever find yourself in Cuzco, make sure you stop at Mercado San Pedro to have their most famous dishes, including Ceviche. There are a lot of hotels, but they tend to be quite expensive, the best option to accomodate will be Airbnb, there is always vacancy and it can be more economical if you are not planning on staying indoors during your visit.

Machu Picchu

This city is the most familiar icon of the Inka Empire, tour operators pick you up in Cuzco and take on a trip that includes a ride on tourist train. The purpose of the city is still a mistery, locals believe it was once the capila of the Inka Empire of a place where royal families could rest and trade. The weather is warm and the best time to visit it is in summer, from May to October. This plase is a sacred symbol to peruvians.
